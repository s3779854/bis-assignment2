User stories and Validation Criteria

Figure Ref. Condition User story Validation Failure condition
1 Enemy is loaded User walks/loads into the screen Enemy is in their position and performing idle behaviour Enemy is not present and is missing on the screen
2 Enemy aggro User walks towards an enemy Enemy reacts to the player’s presence by engaging with the player by either advancing to the player or attacking the player Enemy does not react to the player and does not perform any actions
3 Enemy attack User is in the enemy’s range for attacking Enemies perform their attack pattern Enemy, when aggroed, does not attempt to attack the player
4 Enemy damages player User does not come out of the enemy’s range and therefore gets hit by the enemy attack Enemies inflicts damage onto the player, and the correct amount of damage on the player The enemy does not do any damage to the player even when hitting within the player’s hitbox or does not do the correct amount of damage (heart points)
5 Enemy takes damage User hits the enemy with their weapon When the enemy is hit by the player, the enemy turns red and loses a hit point The enemy’s hitbox is hit however is not registered as damage and/or does not take damage away from the enemy
